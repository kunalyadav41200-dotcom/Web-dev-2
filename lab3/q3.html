What is Free Fire?

Free Fire is a fast-paced battle royale game where 50 players fight to survive on a remote island. The last one standing wins!

Top 3 Characters

  • orion - need 200 EP and protect from any type of damage
  • wukong - which transforms the player into a bush for a set duration

Tips for Beginners

Land in safe zones, collect good loot early, stay in the circle, and always keep moving. Don’t rush unless you’re ready!

The Power of Reading

Reading is a timeless habit that expands our minds, builds empathy, and improves focus. In today’s digital world, picking up a book can be a powerful way to disconnect and grow.

Why Read?

  • To learn and gain knowledge
  • To relax and escape stress
  • To understand others better
  • To improve focus and thinking

Formats Don't Matter

Whether it’s a printed book, an e-book, or an audiobook—what matters most is making reading a habit.

Tips to Read More

  • Read a few minutes daily
  • Carry a book with you
  • Choose topics you enjoy
  • Join a reading group or challenge

Conclusion

Reading nurtures the mind and soul. Make time for it—you’ll be glad you did.
